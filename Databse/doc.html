Article Management System Database Documentation

Tables

  • BaseArticle: Represents base articles with their IDs and names.
  • Dimension: Contains dimensions with their descriptions and titles.
  • Article: Stores articles' sale price, cost, reference, barcode, and associations.

Functions

  • UpdateWarehouseQuantity: A function to update article quantities in warehouses based on operations.
  • IsArticleQuantityAvailable: Checks if article quantities are available in a warehouse for requested operations.

Triggers

  • After_Update_Confirm: A trigger that updates warehouse quantities after confirming an operation.

Views

  • ArticleInfo: Provides information about articles, their base article names, dimensions, sale prices, costs, references, and barcodes.
  • RecentOperations: Displays recent operation details including operation types, actors, warehouses, dates, and confirmations.
